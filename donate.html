Donation Overview

Breast Cancer Hub is a GuideStar Candid Platinum certified and Top-Rated 501(c)(3) Nonprofit, Volunteer Organization, Registered in North Carolina, USA, in September 2017. Our Federal Tax Identification Number is 82-2394795.

Support from "Kindhearted" donors from our community members. Every Penny Counts. Operating expenses are limited to logistics solely needed to execute BCH Goals and Projects. Corporate Matching is applicable - Please check with your Employer.

100% of your donation goes directly to the program you choose. We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and your donation is tax-deductible.

Testimonials

BCH is an extraordinary non profit organization led by an extraordinary individual- Dr Lopa Das Roy. The organization was started by Lopa after a lot of personal sacrifice- in terms of her own career, family and personal growth . She has a single mission - to educate on early detection and prevention of breast cancer and hopefully eradicate cancer from this world with her research work. I feel immensely proud and blessed to be associated with Lopa. BCH transcends all borders and treats every person with equality when it comes to providing support and education to anyone going through their cancer journey. I have been able to connect my close relatives and friends battling cancer and I have to ask them to stop thanking me for getting them into the network of BCH! The best part is the support and informed decisions is all free and just a text away! I wish BCH the very best and may this organization continue to shine and lead the way in eradicating cancer.

- Amrita Borooah, USA

I am totally inspired by the way the whole BCH community works together to remove the cancer related stigmas. Moreover, the support they provide to patients are immeasurable and priceless. I look forward to learn a lot from BCH community and specially from Dr.Lopa. I hope to provide a lot of information and guidance to patients undergoing cancer treatment in my community.
